6  Description
7 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8 Reduce 2D-3D application is a command line interface for the Multi-fragment
9 Registration Library (https://github.com/klepo/libmultifragmentregister),
10 which is a C++ Qt-based library for 2D-3D atlas-based reconstruction
11 and fracture reduction of traumatized long bones.
12 
13 Version from 12 July 2017.
14 
15  Binaries
16 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------
17 The 32-bit binaries with all dependent libraries are available
18 in the "bin/x86/release" folder.
19 
20 
21  Examples
22 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------
23 There are two examples focused on reconstruction of fractured tibia and femur.
24 Please copy the folder "Reduce2D3D_examples" to the "C:/Reduce2D3D_examples"
25 location. Then you can execute the examples by typing:
26 
27 reduce2d3d.exe C:/Reduce2D3D_examples/femur/ reduce.manual.xml

32  Prerequisites
33 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------
34 Qt Toolkit in version 5.x or later is required. Other required librarires are
35 redistributed within the registration library package. The libraries are
36 redistributed in both source and binary form. Following 3rd party libraries are
37 included:
38 
39  * SSIM Renderer (http://www.fit.vutbr.cz/research/prod/?id=458)
40  * Multi-fragment Registration Library (https://github.com/klepo/libmultifragmentregister)
41  * dlib
42  * OpenCV
43  * libmesh
44 
45 The library has been developped for usage with Windows 7 or later.
